AI based Student Attenance System (in progress)

This is an AI-based smart attendance system where students' faces are stored in a database. When a student enters the classroom, their face is detected by a camera at the door, and partial attendance is marked. Upon exiting, their face is detected again, and if the student has spent more than 45 minutes in the classroom, their attendance is confirmed.Attendance records can only be edited by teachers and administrators. Students can join a class on the platform using a unique class ID provided by the class admin, and they must scan their faces themselves for authentication.
